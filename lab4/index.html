Results

Here is the outline to an everyday task

1. Turn on stove
2. Put pan on stove
3. Add butter or oil to pan
4. Add as many eggs to pan
5. Flip eggs if desire
6. Toast bread (two slices)
7. Place egg on one slice of bread
8. Place the other slice of bread on top
9. Eat

Here is the outline for a computer game

1. Dealer shuffles cards
2. Each player gets 2 cards
3. PLAYERS TURN
4. Add sum of cards
5. If sums of cards is less than 21, continue
6. Player can hit or stay
7. If sum is greater than or equal to game is over
7. If sum is 21 or less, continue to dealers turn
8. DEALERS TURN
9. Add sum of cards
10. Check if 17 or greater
11. If yes, dealer must stay
12. If no, dealer must hit
13. If no one busts, whoever has higher total wins
